RenWeb login instructions

    Login:

  • Click on RenWeb in the right column to visit the login page.
  • Type the District Code: fc-sda.
  • Type your User Name: Your first initial and last name (i.e. John Doe’s user name would be jdoe).
  • Type your Password. This should have been sent to you. Click here to request a password.
  • Click the Parent button if it is not already selected.
  • Click Login. The ParentsWeb displays.

    • Update Login Information:

  • In the left column of ParentsWeb click Family Information and then click Username/Password.
  • Type in a new User Name and a new Password, and then retype your new Password.
  • Click Submit.

Click here to contact the school about logging into RenWeb.
