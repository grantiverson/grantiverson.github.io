Who am I?

I have a passion for studying creation which is fueled by my desire to understand God's character. To me, Science–especially Math–proves His existence and demonstrates His creative powers.

My goal in life is to share the love that my Father in Heaven has shown to me. I want others to see Jesus in my life through my actions, words, character, and presence. Everything I do is to bring glory to my God and demonstrate His grace to others. I do everything to the best of my abilities for Him.
